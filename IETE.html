WELCOME TO IETE

The IETE-PCCOE Students Forum operates as a non-profit entity led by students from Pimpri Chinchwad College of Engineering. Our primary mission is to raise awareness among students regarding the latest advancements and emerging trends within the field of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering. We achieve this through various means, including workshops, seminars, speaker sessions, national-level competitions, international conferences, and similar events.
